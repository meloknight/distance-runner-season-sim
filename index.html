Runner Season Simulator

Running Season Info

  • The season consists of a chosen year and runs from the start of March to the end of September.
  • The season is made up of a competitive Conference which contains 20 Teams. Each team is made up of 8 individual Runners.
  • All 160 runners are generated at the start of the season and have numerous characteristics determined for them at the start of the season. These factors are physical, training, nutritional, psychological, biomechanical, and genetic factors.
  • All of these factors are then combined into a "skill class" for each race length. This means that a runner may be better at certain race distances if they have a certain mix of strong factors.
  • From here, a schedule for the season's races is created allocating 2 to 5 races on every second weekend. The runners are then allocated to those races.
  • The runners then have a weather, terrain, and luck modifier added to their skill class in the case the race aligns with their preferred weather and/or terrain. The luck modifier is determined for each race since a runner may feel sick, have an injury, or be in the zone on a race day.
  • Finally, all the races are run, winners are selected by highest raceday skill class, and points are determined. Golds count for 3 points, silvers for 2 points, and bronzes for 1 point.
  • Note: To view full objects with all information included right-click on this webpage and click inspect. From there, select console to view the Conference and Schedule Objects. These contain most information produced by this application.
